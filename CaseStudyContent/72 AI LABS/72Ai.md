# Project: 72 AI Labs

### **Title: 72 AI Labs – Giving a Face to the Code**

**Client:** 72 AI Labs
**Services:** Full Website Development, Brand Identity, Logo Design
**Outcome:** Brand Authority & LinkedIn Growth

---

### **1. The Challenge**

72 AI Labs was doing great automation work, but visually, they were just a name. They needed a brand that looked as professional as their code—clean, corporate, and distinct from the usual "blue brain" AI clichés. They wanted a full digital presence that they could show off immediately.

### **2. The Concept**

The number "72" holds cultural significance for the founders. We wanted to respect that heritage while keeping it relevant to tech.

Instead of random shapes, we used **Islamic Geometry**. The result is the "Celestial Bloom"—a geometric star that also mirrors the nodes of a neural network.

- **The Colors:** We swapped the standard tech blue for **Black, Gold, and White**.
- **The Vibe:** Premium and established, not just another startup.

### **3. The Execution**

We took ownership of the entire build—from the logo design to the full website development.

The timeline was strict: they wanted to go live on the **Prophet Muhammad’s Birthday**. We didn't just meet the deadline; our team moved fast and delivered the fully finished site way ahead of schedule, giving them plenty of time to prepare for their launch.

### **4. The Result**

The rebrand gave them a legitimate face. It allowed them to expand their presence on LinkedIn and approach clients with much more confidence. They evolved from a "group of engineers" into a recognized brand.

**Current Status:**

- **20+** Projects Delivered
- **14+** Happy Clients
- A consistent, professional digital presence.
